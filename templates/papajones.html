Papa Johns Pizza, situated in Southern Village Shops on East Southern Avenue in Tempe, is a popular pizza chain known for its variety of pizzas and sides. Customers frequently enjoy the Pepperoni Pizza, Create Your Own Pizza, Epic Stuffed Crust Pizza, and Garden Fresh Pizza. For sides, Garlic Parmesan Boneless Wings and Garlic Knots are often ordered together. The menu also features a range of Papadias, with options like the Philly Cheesesteak Papadia and Parmesan Crusted BBQ Chicken & Bacon Papadia, alongside a selection of desserts including the OREO Cookie Papa Bites and Double Chocolate Chip Brownie. This location is particularly favored in the evening, reflecting its lively dinner crowd. Less
